Welcome

A Coastal Paradise
in the Heart of Konaseema

Antarvedi is a sacred island where the Vashista Godavari merges with the Bay of Bengal. From the revered Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple to pristine beaches, mangrove trails, and fresh seafood — every moment here is unforgettable.

Spiritual

Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

Built in the 15th–16th century, this Dravidian temple is revered as Dakshina Kashi with a uniquely west-facing deity.

  • 6 AM – 7:30 PM
  • Sakinetipalli Mandal
